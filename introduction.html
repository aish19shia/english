THE AGE OF DISCOVERY

Exploring equals piquancy


"The sea is dangerous and its storms terrible, but these obstacles have never been sufficient reason to remain ashore."

-Ferdinand Magellan


People have always been curious about the world around them, from prehistoric times to the present. Some wished to explore them, while others merely desired to understand their own land.

Voyagers journeyed in the service of a king or a nation, while other explorers went in search of adventure or a new beginning on their own.



Ibn Battutah, Ferdinand Magellan, Francis Drake, and James Cook, traveled throughout the world as it was known to them at the time. Throughout history, explorers have been admired for their courage and spirit.
